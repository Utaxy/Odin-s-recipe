Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and spray a 9x13-inch casserole dish with avocado cooking spray.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and green peppers and cook, stirring, until softened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add garlic and saut√© for 30 seconds.
  3. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook, until no longer pink, breaking up large pieces. Stir and cook until brown, about 5 minutes. Drain excess fat, and reduce the heat to medium-low.
